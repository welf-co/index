Imagination.

I dream of a world where we jump out of bed in the morning, driven by a sense of purpose. A world where we create what's valuable to humanity and meaningful to ourselves. A world where we seek mastery of our talents in the service of our species, this planet and our fellow creatures. A world where we collectively thrive, because we approach life based on the laws of the universe, truths about the flourishing of conscious beings and the workings of the human mind.

Smartphones act as the remote control of our society. Behavioral design grows increasingly more powerful. We can use our scientific understanding of the human mind and behavior to create an universal framework for the actualization of potential.

We can combine behavioral psychology and interaction design to do create social systems that manifest our highest values.

I believe we can create a world of fulfilled potential.
